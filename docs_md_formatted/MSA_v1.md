# Master Services Agreement (MSA)  
**Between:** Aureum Systems, Inc. ("Vendor") and Crestwood Pharmaceuticals Ltd. ("Client")  
**Effective Date:** January 2025  

---

## 1. Definitions

1.1 **Confidential Information:** Any information disclosed by either Party under this Agreement, including business, technical, or regulatory data.  
1.2 **Services:** Vendor shall provide AI-based analytics, model hosting, and data processing services as defined in Exhibit A.  
1.3 **Deliverables:** All reports, models, and software outputs generated by Vendor under this Agreement.  

---

## 2. Term and Termination

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in effect for three (3) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 2.2.  
2.2 Either Party may terminate for cause upon material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice.  

---

## 3. Data Security, Privacy, and Compliance

3.1 Vendor shall implement reasonable security measures to protect Client data, in accordance with industry standards.  
3.2 Client shall provide timely access to regulatory and compliance requirements necessary for Vendor to perform the Services.  
3.3 Both Parties shall comply with applicable data privacy, residency, and export control laws.  
3.4 Vendor may subcontract services to approved third-party vendors; Client shall be notified of critical subcontractors.  

---

## 4. Intellectual Property and Ownership

4.1 Vendor does not retain ownership of its pre-existing IP and models.  
4.2 Client retains ownership of its proprietary datasets.  
4.3 Deliverables incorporating Client data shall be licensed to Client on a limited, non-exclusive basis.  
4.4 Jointly developed models will be subject to separate written agreements.  

---

## 5. Audit and Reporting

5.1 Client may conduct audits of Vendor’s compliance with security, privacy, and regulatory obligations once per year.  
5.2 Vendor shall provide audit logs, documentation, and personnel access as reasonably requested.  
5.3 Both Parties shall maintain documentation for applicable retention periods.  

---

## 6. Incident Response and Breach Notification

6.1 Vendor shall notify Client of any security incident affecting Client data within 48 hours of detection.  
6.2 Client shall cooperate with Vendor in investigation and mitigation efforts.  
6.3 Escalation procedures shall follow mutually agreed timelines.  

---

## 7. Warranties and Representations

7.1 Vendor warrants that the Services shall be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.  
7.2 Client warrants that all data provided to Vendor is legally obtained and does not infringe third-party rights.  

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

8.1 Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.  
8.2 Each Party’s aggregate liability for direct damages shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement in the preceding twelve (12) months.  

---

## 9. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflict of laws.  
9.2 Disputes shall first be addressed via informal negotiation, followed by mediation if unresolved.  

---

## 10. Miscellaneous

10.1 Notices must be in writing and delivered electronically or by certified mail.  
10.2 Amendments must be in writing and signed by authorized representatives.  
10.3 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties regarding the Services.  

---

**Exhibit A – Scope of Services**

- AI analytics platform access, including model hosting, training, and inferencing.  
- Regulatory and compliance reporting tools integrated with Client datasets.  
- Standard support and maintenance services as described in Service Levels.
